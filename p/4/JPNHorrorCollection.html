JAPANESE HORROR COLLECTION
This book explores traditional Japanese myths and legends alongside contemporary uses of Japanese horror in today's movies, films, and folklore.
Timeline: Decemeber 2022
Programs: InDesign, Photoshop, After Effects, Unity3D
Role: Graphic/Motion Designer
CONCEPT
The book contains 3 main chapters where the aesthetic of the pages reflect the content being displayed. The sleeve for the book is a collage of both traditional Japanese objects alongside objects seen in horror media. The pages are bound by Japanese stab stitch.
