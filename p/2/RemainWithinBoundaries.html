Remain Within Boundaries
A book and motion graphic that explores the various entryways in NYC by categorizing them in regards to public or private spaces.
Timeline: September 2023
Programs: InDesign, Photoshop, After Effects
Role: Graphic/Motion Designer
Concept
While walking around NYC, I captured 50 images of various open and closed doors in the city. Even though some of the doors I captured were open to the public, they ended up being entryways to private spaces. It made me question what makes a space feel inviting when the line between public and private space is not clearly defined.
